FillFaker Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

TL;DR: FillFaker does not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data. Everything happens locally in your browser.

1. Information We Collect

None. FillFaker does not collect any personal information, browsing data, form contents, or usage analytics. The extension generates fake test data entirely within your browser and never sends any data to external servers.

2. Permissions & Why We Need Them

FillFaker requests the following browser permissions:

  • Active Tab / All URLs — Required to inject content scripts that detect form fields on web pages and fill them with generated fake data. The extension does not read, collect, or transmit any existing form data or page content.
  • Storage (chrome.storage.local) — Used to store three values locally on your device: your usage count, paid/unpaid status, and your "only fill empty fields" preference. This data never leaves your browser.

3. Data Storage

All data stored by FillFaker resides exclusively in your browser's local storage via chrome.storage.local. Specifically:

  • Usage count (number of times the fill action has been used)
  • Paid status (whether the extension has been unlocked)
  • "Only fill empty fields" preference

No data is stored on any remote server. You can clear this data at any time by uninstalling the extension or clearing extension data in your browser settings.

4. Third-Party Services

FillFaker uses ExtensionPay (extensionpay.com) solely to process one-time payments for unlocking the full version. ExtensionPay handles payment processing independently; FillFaker does not receive or store any payment details, billing information, or account credentials. Please refer to ExtensionPay's privacy policy for details on how they handle payment data.

FillFaker does not integrate any analytics services, tracking pixels, advertising networks, or any other third-party code beyond ExtensionPay.

5. Cookies & Tracking

FillFaker does not use cookies, fingerprinting, or any tracking mechanism whatsoever.

6. Data Sharing

Since FillFaker collects no user data, there is no data to share, sell, or disclose to any third party.

7. Children's Privacy

FillFaker does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
